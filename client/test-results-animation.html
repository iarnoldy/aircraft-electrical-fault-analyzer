⚠️ Safety Warnings

  • Disconnect battery before performing electrical work
  • Use insulated tools when testing alternator output
  • Do not operate aircraft with known electrical faults

📋 Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Check battery voltage at rest (should be 12.6V for 12V system)
  2. Verify alternator output voltage with engine running (14.2-14.4V)
  3. Inspect main bus voltage under load conditions
  4. Test circuit breakers for continuity and proper operation
  5. Examine wiring harness for corrosion or damage

💡 Recommendations

  • Replace battery if voltage drops below 11.8V at rest
  • Clean and tighten all electrical connections
  • Document findings in aircraft maintenance log
  • Retest system after repairs to verify proper operation
